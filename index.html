AboutUs

TBN is a Fusion and Classical Competition. There will be 4 teams of each type, competing within their category for a cash prize of $1000. The three awards are:

First Place (Fusion) - $1000 cash prize

First Place (Classical) - $1000 cash prize

Viewer’s Choice Award (Either Classical or Fusion)
